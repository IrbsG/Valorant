COMPETITIVE RANK

There are 9 ranks in VALORANT; Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Ascendant, Immortal, and Radiant. Ranks Iron to Immortal are divided into three tiers from Tier 1 to Tier 3, with Tier 3 being the highest tier within a rank. Players ranked in Immortal and Radiant are featured on a leaderboard.

Get Ready for the Finale of the 2025 VCT Season!

Today we're thrilled to announce the initial details for Champions Paris! Champions will bring together the entire VALORANT community and the best professional teams in the world for the biggest event of the year! To qualify for the event, top teams from the 4 International Leagues battled through a season-long gauntlet of regular season matches and global events to claim one of the sixteen available spots. From September 12–October 5, the best of the best will converge in France to compete for the Champions trophy and the title of VALORANT World Champion!
